About

Building digital access from hardware to interface.

Open Microlock started from the need to make digital access systems that do not stop at demos. The community connects firmware, mobile apps, dashboards, documentation, and contribution processes so the work can be learned and improved openly.

Open by design Repositories, documentation, and roadmaps are prepared for public access.
Real prototypes Focused on devices, applications, and workflows that can be tested.
Community first Contributions can come through firmware, mobile, backend, UI/UX, and docs.

DARMI

One interface for a digital access ecosystem.

01 ESP32 Devices Smart lock, smart lamp, simulator.
02 Local API HTTP JSON endpoints for prototyping.
03 Mobile & Dashboard Device control and operational monitoring.
04 Users & Operations Users, communities, makers, and local programs.
